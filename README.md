# 🚀 ZyroSafeAI 
### Hyperlocal Income Insurance for Grocery Delivery Partners  

---

## 📌 Problem Statement  

Grocery delivery partners (Blinkit, Zepto) depend on daily deliveries for income.  
However, external disruptions like heavy rain, flooding, and traffic congestion reduce their working hours and cause significant income loss.  

These disruptions are unpredictable and beyond their control, yet there is currently no system to protect their income.  

---

## 👤 Persona Scenario  

Meet Ravi, a Blinkit delivery partner in Delhi.  
He works 8 hours daily and earns ₹400–₹500 per day.  

During heavy rainfall:  
- Orders drop significantly  
- Delivery becomes difficult or impossible  
- His daily income becomes ₹0  

This loss is not due to lack of effort, but due to external conditions.  

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution  

QuickShield AI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed specifically for grocery delivery partners.  

It:  
- Monitors hyperlocal weather and traffic conditions  
- Detects disruptions automatically  
- Calculates potential income loss  
- Provides instant payouts without manual claims  

👉 Focus: **Income loss protection only (no health, vehicle, or accident coverage)**  

---

## ⚙️ System Workflow  

1. Rider onboarding (enter details, select zone)  
2. AI-based risk profiling  
3. Weekly plan selection  
4. Real-time monitoring (weather & traffic APIs)  
5. Disruption detection (parametric triggers)  
6. Validation (location, activity, duplicate checks)  
7. Instant payout

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers  

| Disruption | Trigger Condition |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Heavy Rain | Rainfall > 50 mm |
| Flood | Flood alert in zone |
| Heatwave | Temperature > 45°C |
| Pollution | AQI > 300 |
| Traffic | Speed < 10 km/h |

👉 If conditions are met → **Automatic payout triggered**  

---

## 🤖 AI/ML Integration  

### 1. Risk Assessment  
- Uses weather, traffic, and zone data  
- Generates risk score  
- Determines weekly premium  

### 2. Predictive Modeling  
- Identifies high-risk zones and time slots  
- Estimates income loss probability  

### 3. Fraud Detection  
- GPS validation  
- Rider activity check  
- Duplicate claim prevention  
- Anomaly detection  

---

## 🔐 Fraud Prevention Logic  

- Rider must be present in the disruption zone  
- Rider must be active/online  
- No duplicate claims allowed  
- Suspicious patterns flagged  

---

## 💸 Payout Logic  

Payout is calculated based on:  

Payout = Avg Hourly Income × Affected Hours × Severity Multiplier  

Example:  
₹80/hour × 2 hours × 1.5 = ₹240
